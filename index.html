Our Mission

Kerigami is born from two ideas: Kerygma — a proclamation of good news — and Origami — the art of transforming something simple into something extraordinary.

We celebrate the intricate beauty of the neurodivergent mind. Every fold matters. Every brain is a masterpiece. Our apps are designed with ADHD families at the center — clear, rewarding, and built for focus.

Our Story

Kerigami was founded by Welbr, a children’s mental health counselor turned stay-at-home father, alongside his wife, Erika, a business executive with a background in strategy and leadership.

After years of helping families navigate challenges like focus, organization, and emotional regulation, Welbr saw a consistent gap—what worked in theory often broke down in everyday life.

Now raising two boys in Middle Tennessee, both he and his son experience the unique strengths and challenges that come with ADHD. Living it daily—not just studying it—shaped a deeper understanding of what families actually need.

Together, Welbr and Erika set out to build what they couldn’t find: simple, practical tools that work in the real rhythms of family life.

Kerigami bridges the gap between clinical insight and lived experience, creating digital structures designed to help neurodivergent minds—and the families who support them—thrive.
