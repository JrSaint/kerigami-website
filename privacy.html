Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 16, 2026

Introduction

Kerigami LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information of our users. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data when you use our applications, including Kids Chore Chart.

Information We Collect

When you use Kids Chore Chart, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information — email address and password, used for authentication.
  • Family profile data — parent-created child names, quest/chore titles, reward names, and XP balances stored to operate the app.
  • Usage data — anonymized interaction data (e.g., feature usage frequency) to help us improve the app experience.

We do not collect precise geolocation, contacts, photos, or any biometric data.

How We Use Your Data

We use collected information solely to:

  • Provide and operate the Kids Chore Chart app and its features.
  • Authenticate your account and keep your family data secure.
  • Improve app performance, stability, and user experience.
  • Communicate important service updates or respond to support requests.

We Do Not Sell Your Data

Kerigami LLC does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers. Your family's information is yours.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard cloud infrastructure with encryption in transit and at rest. We use Supabase as our backend provider, which employs row-level security policies to ensure that each family can only access their own data.

Children's Privacy

Kids Chore Chart is designed for family use. Child profiles are created and managed exclusively by an authenticated parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. All child data is entered and controlled by the parent account holder.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Supabase — authentication and database hosting.

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your account and associated family data at any time.
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Withdraw consent and close your account.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@kerigami.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of our apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
